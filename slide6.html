Decarbonizing an Industry That Never Slows Down

Terra Carbon Development is a sustainable industrial technology company focused on one of the hardest sectors to decarbonize: steel. Its proprietary Carbon Refinery™ technology combines fluid bed pyrolysis with commercial off-the-shelf systems to produce green pig iron — a lower-carbon, cost-competitive alternative to the material steelmakers already rely on.

The same process opens the door to sustainable aviation fuel, blue methanol, and other carbon-neutral co-products, giving Terra Carbon a platform that extends well beyond steel. Our job was to give that technical story a website confident enough for investors and clear enough for anyone hearing it for the first time.

The Refinery

The Carbon Refinery™ is Terra Carbon's flagship system — integrating proven fluid bed pyrolysis with commercial off-the-shelf equipment to convert subbituminous coal and iron ore into green pig iron, while routing captured CO2 into carbon-neutral co-products instead of the atmosphere. The company's first facility is planned for Colombia, targeting 1 million tonnes of green pig iron per year.

The Positioning

We treated the technology itself as the hero — translating a genuinely complex industrial process into a website that reads clearly for an investor evaluating the opportunity and an engineer scrutinizing the specs.

Content strategy built for two audiences: a clean narrative arc for sustainability advocates and press, paired with the technical depth — emissions figures, production capacity, technology readiness — that investors and industry partners expect to see.

How We Built It

The Deep-Tech Site Framework

Terra Carbon's audience ranges from sustainability journalists to project financiers. This is the four-step framework we used to build one site that serves both without diluting the technology.

01

Technical Discovery

We sat with the process itself — the pyrolysis step, the smelt reaction, the co-product streams — until we understood it well enough to simplify it without distorting it.

02

Audience Mapping

We separated the site's two real audiences — investors doing diligence and advocates evaluating impact — and built a narrative path that serves both without forcing either to dig.

03

Visual System Design

A clean, modern design language — diagrams, facility renderings, and clear data — gave the technology the same credibility on screen that it has on paper.

04

Scalable Build

We built the site to grow with the company — ready for new facilities, new co-products, and new data as the first Carbon Refinery™ moves from plan to groundbreaking.
