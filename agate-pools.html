Agate Pools: A Masterclass in Submerged Luxury

Redefining the residential oasis through geological precision and architectural integration.

The Paradox of Depth

In the realm of elite residential design, the pool has long been a functional afterthought—a tile-lined rectangle of utility. Hathaway Strategic sought to invert this paradigm. Our vision for Agate Pools was to bridge the gap between raw natural textures and modern luxury living.

"We didn't just build a pool; we curated a geological event. By using semi-precious stone as the primary skin, we created an immersive environment that shifts with the angle of the sun, offering a serene, meditative experience of optical clarity."
